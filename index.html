I'm a Software Engineer at a startup gaming company in Las Vegas, leveraging my expertise to build innovative and engaging digital experiences.

A proud Graduate of the University of Nevada Las Vegas with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, I specialize in full-stack development with a passion for creating responsive UIs and robust backend systems. My technical toolkit includes JavaScript, Python, and C++, among other modern technologies.

Previously, I contributed as a former Academic Tutor at the College of Southern Nevada, guiding students through complex CS concepts. I also maintain a role as a resilient lead in the Drumbots, the acclaimed official NHL drumline for the Vegas Golden Knights.

Driven by the rapid evolution of technology, I aim to develop cutting-edge software solutions that stand at the intersection of innovation and user experience.
